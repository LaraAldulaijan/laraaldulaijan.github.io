Who we are

We're a group from TUM and LMU, passionate about UI/UX. Taking matters into our own hands, we've created a community with a mission: to collaborate, learn, and grow together in the field of UI/UX. We are accredited by TUM since November 2023.

What we do

We learn User Experience and design thinking principles. Then we use that new knowledge and apply it through our projects. We start with understanding the user and end in beautiful designs.

Do you have a project for us?

We take new projects between each semester. Feel free to contact us via our email info@tudesignclub.de or our social media accounts. Tell us about what kind of work you do, your project and make sure to mention any requirements on your side. We look forward to working with you!
